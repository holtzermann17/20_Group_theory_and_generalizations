Jordan-Hölder decomposition


A Jordan–Hölder decomposition of a group G is a filtration

G=G1⊃G2⊃⋯⊃Gn={1}

such that Gi+1 is a normal subgroup of Gi and the quotient Gi/Gi+1 is a simple group for each i.
